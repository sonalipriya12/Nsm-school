Welcome to NSM Alumni Association

About NSM

NSM Alumni Association brings together graduates across generations to celebrate achievements, share opportunities, and support the growth of NSM College.

Stay connected with your batchmates, faculty and the broader NSM community through events, networking and mentoring initiatives.

  • Regional chapter meets and professional networking.

About NSM Alumni Association

NSM Alumni Association was officially started in 1990 with the vision of building a vibrant community of graduates who support each other and their alma mater. At present there are 34,000+ members.

NSM College aims at the training of young men and women of quality to be leaders in all walks of life and to serve society in justice, truth and service. It is expected that this training will play a vital role in bringing about a just social order in our country.

NSM Connect

NSM Connect is a platform provided by the NSM Alumni Association to enable alumni across the globe to stay connected, collaborate, and contribute to the growth of the NSM community.

Sign in to access exclusive alumni features, participate in events, mentor students, and support ongoing projects at NSM College.

NSM Alumni Day

NSM Alumni Day is celebrated every year on the first Sunday of August. Graduates from across the globe return to campus to reconnect with faculty, friends and the NSM spirit.

The day features keynote addresses, cultural performances, recognition of distinguished alumni, and opportunities to engage with current students.

NSM Alumni Events

Career Connect 2025

An exclusive networking event where NSM alumni share industry insights, mentor final-year students and explore collaboration opportunities.

Global Chapters Meet

Regional alumni chapters host meetups in major cities worldwide, strengthening the NSM network beyond campus.

Batch Re-Unions

NSM encourages every graduating batch to organize regular reunions on campus and across cities. Reunions are a perfect occasion to relive memories, meet professors and celebrate shared milestones.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as an NSM alumnus?

Click on the Sign In button at the top-right and follow the registration process. Once approved, you will receive login credentials via email.

How can I contribute to NSM?

You can contribute through scholarships, infrastructure support, student mentoring or by hosting events. Reach out via the Contact Us page for more details.

Contact NSM Alumni Association

For any queries related to alumni registration, events or contributions, please write to us or call the NSM Alumni Office.
