About NSM Alumni Association

NSM Alumni Association was officially started in 1990 with the vision of building a vibrant community of graduates who support each other and their alma mater. At present there are 34,000+ members.

NSM College aims at the training of young men and women of quality to be leaders in all walks of life and to serve society in justice, truth and service. It is expected that this training will play a vital role in bringing about a just social order in our country.

President's Message

Dear NSM Alumni Family,

It is with immense pleasure and pride that I address you as the President of the NSM Alumni Association. As we continue to strengthen the bonds that connect us across generations, I am inspired by the dedication and achievements of our alumni community.

NSM College has been a beacon of excellence in education for over a century, nurturing young minds to become leaders, innovators, and compassionate individuals who make meaningful contributions to society. Our alumni have excelled in diverse fields—from business and technology to arts and public service—and continue to make us proud with their accomplishments.

The Alumni Association serves as a bridge between our rich past and promising future. Through our various initiatives, networking events, and mentorship programs, we strive to create opportunities for alumni to connect, collaborate, and contribute to the growth of our alma mater and the betterment of society.

I encourage each one of you to actively participate in our events, share your experiences, and mentor the next generation of NSM students. Together, we can build a stronger, more vibrant alumni network that honors our heritage while embracing innovation and progress.

Let us continue to uphold the values of justice, truth, and service that NSM instilled in us. Your engagement, support, and commitment are the pillars that strengthen our community.

Warm regards,
Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Benefits of an Alumni

As a member of the NSM Alumni Association, you gain access to a wide range of benefits designed to support your professional growth, expand your network, and help you stay connected with your alma mater and fellow graduates.

Wider Professional Network

Alumni associations are rife with opportunities for expanding a graduate's professional network. Connect with fellow alumni across various industries, sectors, and geographic locations to build meaningful professional relationships.

Targeted Career Services

Alumni associations often provide a wealth of career services to help former students find job opportunities and improve their chances of landing a job offer. Career fairs bring together employers from around the area, and sometimes further, so that graduates can meet company representatives face-to-face. Career counseling, seminars, webinars, and networking events may provide helpful information about the job market. Similarly, an association may offer resume and cover letter assistance to help graduates put their best foot forward.

Access to College Resources Post Graduation

In addition to career services, alumni associations may provide various resources to help former students post-graduation. This might include access to library materials, extensive journal and periodical databases, and even educational amenities on campus, such as certain labs and equipment.

Contact with Classmates

Being able to connect with former classmates can be another benefit to belonging to an alumni association. Whether this leads to a career opportunity or is simply a chance to catch up with old friends and acquaintances, graduates will benefit from alumni directories and online social media groups that keep the lines of communication open well after the caps and gowns have been removed.

Connect with Professors/Parent Departments

Connecting fellow alumni is one way to find career and social opportunities, but reconnecting with past professors can offer a wealth of opportunities as well. This is particularly true for research, as many professors at colleges and universities are engaged in some type of academic research. Graduates could connect with professors in their field to gain worthwhile experience or guidance with a particular research topic.

Connect with the Community

Many associations partner with their local community so that alumni can provide assistance to the area while also having the opportunity to build relationships with community members and leaders.

Socializing

Alumni associations aren't always all business. They offer several chances to partake in social events, such as mixers, happy hours, and galas as well as homecoming events, football tailgates, alumni award ceremonies, and class reunions. Graduates can take a break from work and post-college life and reconnect with former classmates as well as other alumni, while making social connections that could lead to lasting friendships.

Giving Back

Graduates often want to stay connected to their alma mater and help future generations of alumni have the same opportunities and positive experiences they did. Alumni associations offer a number of ways to give back, including gifts, endowments, membership fees, and volunteer opportunities. Giving back to an alumni association is a great way to keep the school's legacy going and ensure that future generations of students have the opportunity to pursue quality higher education.

About NSM Alumni Day (NAD)

Celebrating Unity, Excellence, and Legacy

An Annual Tradition of Unity

NSM Alumni Day (NAD) stands as one of the most significant and eagerly anticipated events in the NSM calendar. Celebrated annually on the first Sunday of August, this grand gathering brings together thousands of alumni from across the globe, transcending generations, borders, and professional fields. The day serves as a powerful reminder of the enduring bonds that connect NSM graduates, regardless of when they walked the hallowed halls of the institution.

Reconnecting with the NSM Spirit

For many alumni, NSM Alumni Day represents a homecoming – an opportunity to return to the place where their professional journeys began, to reconnect with former classmates, mentors, and professors, and to relive the memories that shaped their formative years. The campus comes alive with laughter, stories, and the palpable sense of belonging that defines the NSM community. It is a day where the past meets the present, where experienced professionals share wisdom with emerging leaders, and where the spirit of NSM is renewed and strengthened.

Celebrating Achievements and Excellence

The day is marked by a rich tapestry of events designed to celebrate achievements, honor distinguished alumni, and inspire future generations. Keynote addresses by eminent alumni who have made significant contributions to their fields provide insights and inspiration. Recognition ceremonies celebrate outstanding accomplishments across various domains – from business excellence and academic achievements to community service and lifetime contributions. Cultural performances showcase the diverse talents within the NSM family, creating an atmosphere of celebration and joy.

Building Bridges for the Future

NSM Alumni Day is not just a celebration of the past, but a forward-looking event that builds bridges between generations. Current students have the unique opportunity to interact with successful alumni, gaining valuable insights into career paths, industry trends, and professional development. Mentorship sessions, networking opportunities, and interactive workshops facilitate meaningful connections that extend beyond the day itself. These interactions often lead to internships, job opportunities, and lifelong professional relationships that benefit both students and alumni.

Contributing to NSM's Growth

The day also serves as a platform for alumni to give back to their alma mater. Through various initiatives, contributions, and collaborative projects, alumni demonstrate their commitment to ensuring that future generations of NSM students have access to even better opportunities and resources. Whether through scholarship programs, infrastructure development, research funding, or mentorship, NSM Alumni Day becomes a catalyst for positive change and institutional growth. It embodies the principle that NSM alumni are not just products of the institution, but active partners in its continued evolution and excellence.

About Reunion

Celebrating Together

NSM encourages every graduating batch to organize regular reunions on campus and across cities. Reunions are a perfect occasion to relive memories, meet professors, and celebrate shared milestones. These gatherings strengthen the bond between alumni and their alma mater, creating lasting connections that span generations.

Annual Reunion Events

Each year, NSM Alumni Association hosts multiple reunion events bringing together graduates from different batches. These events feature campus tours, interactive sessions with faculty, networking opportunities, and cultural programs. Alumni share their professional journeys, mentor current students, and contribute to the college's growth.

Building Connections

Reunions serve as a platform for alumni to reconnect with old friends, make new connections, and stay engaged with the NSM community. Whether it's a small batch gathering or a grand alumni meet, these events create cherished memories and reinforce the spirit of camaraderie that defines the NSM family.

Get Involved

If you're interested in organizing a reunion for your batch or participating in upcoming reunion events, please contact the NSM Alumni Office. We provide full support for planning and organizing reunions, ensuring memorable experiences for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as an NSM alumnus?

Click on the Sign In button at the top-right and follow the registration process. Once approved, you will receive login credentials via email.

How can I contribute to NSM?

You can contribute through scholarships, infrastructure support, student mentoring or by hosting events. Reach out via the Contact Us page for more details.

For any queries related to alumni registration, events or contributions, please write to us or call the NSM Alumni Office.

About NSM

NSM College aims at the training of young men and women of quality to be leaders in all walks of life and to serve society in justice, truth and service. It is expected that this training will play a vital role in bringing about a just social order in our country.
